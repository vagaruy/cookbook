<h3>spicy Scrambed Egg Recipe</h3>

http://www.seriouseats.com/recipes/2012/02/anda-bhurji-spicy-indian-scrambled-eggs-recipe.html

* Servings: 2
* Prep Time:  10 mins
* Cook Time:  50 mins
* Total Time: 70 mins

Ingredients:

* 6 Eggs
* 1 Medium Onion
* 2  Tomato
* 4 green chillies
* 1 tablespoon ginger garlic paste
* A bunch of cilantro
* 4 Curry Leaves
* Paw Bhaji Masala
* Turmeric
* Chilli Powder
* Curry Powder
* Oil and Butter
* Salt to taste

Directions:

1. Finely dice the onions , green chillies and tomato. Chop some cilantro leaves and pick a few curry leaves.
2. Heat 2 Tbsp of oil and cook the onions till they soften.
3. Cook the tomatoes and green chillies till softened. Also add the curry leaves.
4. Once cooked. add the Paw Bhaji Masala, turmeric, chilli powder and curry powder.
5. Add the ginger garlic paste and cook till the spices are infused and the raw aroma goes away.
6. Whisk the eggs and add salt to it. Cook it with the mixture till the eggs curdle.
7. Adjust the spices according to taste and add a tablespoon of butter for flavoring.
13. Serve with chopped cilantro
